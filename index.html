Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of wax do I use?

Hard wax. Why? Because it adheres to the hairs more than it does the skin so it makes it more effective and in my opinion less painful

What should I wear the day of my visit?

Anything that will make you comfortable! You will be most comfortable post-wax in looser clothing but it's up to you

Can I work out or have sex after getting waxed?

It is best to wait 48 hours after waxing before any strenuous activity to avoid developing a rash.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please provide a 24 hour notice for a cancellation. No-show and same-day cancellations will be charged 50% of the service booked to the card number with which they were held.

Do men get waxed?

Most definitely! I offer Manzilians (the male version of the Brazilian), and many men also wax their chests, backs, noses and ears.

I just started my period, can I still get waxed?

It is okay to get waxed when on your period. Please be sure to change your tampon right before your appointment. Also remember the increase of blood flow may make your skin more sensitive.

What should I do for homecare after getting waxed?

After the 48 hr period has passed, be sure to moisturize daily. Exfoliate at least twice a week to help prevent ingrown hairs.
